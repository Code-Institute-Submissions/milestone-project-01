studes (Latin)

studeo, studes, studere E, studui
1. to direct one's zeal
2. to be eager for
The Studes will be a membership based co-work destination that provides facilities for designers, multimedia artists, makers, craft people and digital creatives to be at their creative best.
It will encourage collaboration and community by bringing people together from a broad spectrum of creative disciplines.
Private work spaces and meeting rooms will be made available to all community members giving them the opportunity to maintain a professional working relationship with their clients when needed.
It will provide facility access on a membership tier system that allows varying levels of involvement.
Specialist materials will be made available for use.
