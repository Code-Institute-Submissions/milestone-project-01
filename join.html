why become a member?

To access the range of facilities at the studes.

To be represented by the studes agency.

To join the Stockholm's creative community network.

As we know that our members are different and won't use the space the same way, which is why we offer 3 different tiers of membership to be as flexible as possible.

Below is some more information about the different tiers of membership and all the amazing things that come with it!

Tier 1 Membership

  • Monthly Fee
  • Pay-as-you-go for facilities
  • Access to the members directory
  • Representation by the studes agency
Apply for Tier 1 Membership

Tier 2 Membership

  • Monthly Fee
  • Storage locker (so you don't need to carry all those materials around with you!)
  • Priority booking for facilities
  • Pay-as-you-go for facilities
  • Access to the members directory
  • Representation by the studes agency
Apply for Tier 2 Membership

Tier 3 Membership

  • Monthly Fee
  • Unlimited use of facilities
  • Storage locker (so you don't need to carry all those materials around with you!)
  • Priority booking for facilities
  • Access to the members directory
  • Representation by the studes agency
Apply for Tier 3 Membership

the studes Day Pass

  • Membership for a single day
  • Unlimited use of facilities
  • Priority booking for facilities
  • Access to the members directory
  • Available to buy at the reception
